About Me

Hi, I’m a Master’s student in Physics who enjoys asking questions and figuring things out as I go. Someone once told me that science has an answer for everything, and that idea stuck with me. When I’m not working on my studies, I like playing the guitar and jamming with friends, spending time reading and writing, and experimenting in the kitchen—sometimes successfully, sometimes just for fun.

During my undergraduate days, I was part of the astronomy club, where I discovered how enjoyable it is to explore ideas and share them with others. At my current institution, I’m working on building a student-led, science-oriented society that encourages open conversation, curiosity, and a strong scientific temperament, helping create an environment where students feel comfortable asking questions and learning together.
